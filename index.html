Who I Am

Katy Luxem is a writer, editor, and entrepreneur living in Salt Lake City, UT. As a poet and storyteller, Katy has work in Rattle, McSweeney's, HAD, The National Poetry Review, Poetry Online, SWWIM Every Day, The Appalachian Review, and others. Her first book, Until It Is True, was published in 2023 by Kelsay Books.

As a professional writer and content editor, Katy has written copy and led marketing for small, local businesses and large, international brands. These include Amazon, Ocean Spray, Panasonic, Cole Haan, Rubbermaid, Sharpie, Graco, Honeywell, Nilfisk, ISLE Surf & SUP, and more.

Since 2020, Katy has worked as the founder and CEO of Big Dill Pickleball Co. which has been featured in Forbes, The New York Times, Men's Health, Parade, Good Housekeeping, and more.
